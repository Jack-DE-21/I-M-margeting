Brand idea

Where the brand sits

Positioning

Not cheap fast food. Not premium fine dining. The gap in the middle: modern, warm, and accessible.

Core offer

European street food, grill dishes, salads, sides, drinks, and desserts across dine-in, takeaway, and delivery.

Why it works

Customers want value, speed, hot food, and a late-night option that still feels higher quality than standard takeaway.

Menu structure

Simple menu, strong add-ons, easier ordering

Core mains

High-demand items like grill plates, kebabs, and fast-repeat comfort food.

Shareable late-night picks

Easy group ordering, quick choices, and bundles that increase basket size.

Comfort + quality

Food that feels hearty and familiar, but presented with stronger branding and better experience.

Bundle idea: main + dessert + drink for fast checkout and higher average order value.

Designed to pull first-time orders

Launch Offer

Get a free dessert add-on with any main meal ordered directly through the EMBER & CO. website. This offer supports first-time trial and encourages customers to order directly instead of relying only on delivery apps.

Code: EMBERDESSERT

Why this offer was chosen

Survey findings showed strong interest in dessert add-ons priced between €3–5. This made dessert the most suitable incentive for the digital campaign.
