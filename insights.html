What customers actually told us

This page turns the market research into clear customer insights that guide the digital marketing campaign for EMBER & CO. Kitchen.

Survey-backed decisions

Key headline findings

18–24was the strongest age group
Weeklytakeaway was most common overall
Late nightand dinner were the top demand windows
Delivery appswere the most used ordering method
€13–18was the preferred meal price range
€3–5was the strongest dessert add-on range

Open response themes

What customers care about most

Affordable pricing

Customers repeatedly mentioned cheap, fair, or better-value food as a major decision factor.

Fast service

Waiting times and service speed came up often, especially for late-night food occasions.

Food arriving hot

Customers want delivery and takeaway meals to stay warm and feel freshly prepared.

What this means

How insights shape the campaign

Target audience focus

The campaign should focus mainly on students and young adults, as this was the strongest market segment in the research.

Channel choice

Instagram and TikTok should be prioritised for awareness and offers because respondents preferred these platforms most.

Offer design

Value meals, dessert add-ons, and late-night promotions match the strongest survey patterns and purchase drivers.
